Aadhaar Banking

Aadhaar Enabled Payment Services (AEPS)

Infinity Clik offers the opportunity for any AEPS agent to transform their store into an Aadhaar ATM. By using their Aadhaar card and thumb authentication, customers can securely withdraw cash, obtain a mini statement, check their account balance, and even make payments. As an AEPS agent with Infinity Clik , you can earn a substantial commission for each transaction performed through the Aadhaar ATM.

Mini ATM

Convert your Store into ATM

Infinity Clik 's Mini ATM machine provides an excellent solution for merchants who desire to receive card payments anytime, anywhere, while also providing additional services to their customers. By using the Micro ATM machine, you can transform your store into an ATM and offer cash withdrawal services directly to your customers.

Money Transfer

Domestic Money Transfer (DMT)

Infinity Clik offers a fantastic opportunity for entrepreneurs to start a money transfer business. As a Infinity Clik agent, you can transfer cash from your outlet to any bank account across the country, with the lowest charges in the industry, resulting in a high-income opportunity. With each transaction, you, as a retailer, can earn a substantial income.

Payment Solutions

UPI, QR Code, MPOS, Aadhar Pay

Infinity Clik partners can provide their customers with various payment options for their convenience, including a unique UPI & QR Code that customers can scan to make payments. In addition, MPOS is available for debit/credit card swipes, payment links for online payments, and Aadhaar Pay, which enables customers to pay using their Aadhaar card, among many other payment options.

Utility Services

Recharge, Bill Payments, PAN Card

Infinity Clik provides its partners with a wide range of services, including customer mobile and DTH recharge, electricity and gas bill payments, as well as PAN card creation services. By offering these services, partners can attract more customers and expand their service portfolio while earning money on each transaction.

Bank Accounts and Loans

Risk Free Guaranteed Returns

Infinity Clik offers a risk-free, guaranteed return insurance savings plan with a 25 times cover, providing greater returns than any standard savings account. As a Infinity Clik partner, you can increase your income and help customers achieve their savings goals with this plan.

Credit card apply

Credit Card without Account

Infinity Clik partners can provide customers without a bank account with a debit card by using the Infinity Clik Prepaid card. Customers can use this card to make purchases just like a standard debit card.
